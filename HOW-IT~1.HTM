A daily rhythm, not another dashboard.

Legacy Coach isn't a tool you have to remember to open. It shows up three times — morning, evening, and once a week — the way a real coach would, turning ambition into momentum you can feel.

01Every morning
☀️

Set the day

A two-minute session turns your goals into three concrete moves for today. No blank page, no overthinking — just the next right actions, ranked.

02Every evening
🌙

Reflect & close

An evening check-in reviews what got done, names what got in the way, and protects tomorrow's focus. Wins logged; excuses surfaced — gently.

03Every week
🧭

Zoom out

A weekly review connects seven days of effort back to your real goals, recalibrates priorities, and keeps the big arc on track.

A day with your coach

Two minutes that change how the day goes.

You don't open an app and stare at a blank dashboard. Your coach greets you, references yesterday, and helps you choose the one move that matters most today.

  • ✓ Morning: pick today's three moves in under two minutes
  • ✓ Midday: a light nudge if you've drifted
  • ✓ Evening: close the loop, log the win, protect tomorrow
