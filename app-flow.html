Complete app flow

Every screen in Legacy Coach and how they connect — from first sign-up through everyday use.

How to read this→ tap to move to the next screenCaption above each arrow = the button you tap↳ an option on that screen

1Get in — sign up & verify

New users start at Sign up with name, email, and password (or Continue with Google). A 4-digit code verifies the email, then the app moves straight into onboarding. Returning users log in instead, and can reset a forgotten password from the same screen.

2Onboarding — meet your coach

Onboarding is a short guided chat. The coach introduces itself, asks a few questions about you and how you work, then shows a profile summary (personality, interests, and more). Tapping Let’s Go drops you into the app. The same flow also has a dark-mode version.

3Set your first goal

With no active goal, the app prompts you to create one. Your goal is the spine of the experience — it drives which sessions are recommended and powers your streak, days-left, and momentum tracking.

No goal yet

4Your daily hub — the Home tab

Home is your daily command center. At a glance you see your active goal, the session recommended for right now, today’s commitments, and a quick mood check. Tapping the recommended session opens a guided coaching chat.

Home
Recommended session
→
Coaching session
Options on the Home screen
  • ↳Goal card — streak, days left, and momentum
  • ↳Recommended session — opens the right session for the time of day
  • ↳Today’s commitments — your moves for the day; check each off
  • ↳Today’s Pulse — log how you feel (Rough → Flow); your coach references it next session

6Account, credits & history

From your profile you manage your account — top up credits, change your plan, set language or dark mode, and find privacy controls. Session History keeps a running log of every commitment.

  • ↳Privacy — policy & export your data; and Logout
Session history

Session History groups every commitment by date so you can look back on what you’ve done across sessions.
