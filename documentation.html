Create your account

Setting up takes under a minute.

  1. Open the app and tap Sign Up (or tap Log In if you already have an account).
  2. Enter your full name, email, and a password — then confirm the password.
  3. Tap Create Account. Prefer Google? Tap Continue with Google instead.
  4. Next, you'll verify your email.

Verify your email

A quick 4-digit code confirms it's really you.

  1. Check your inbox for the verification code.
  2. Type the 4 digits into the boxes on the Verification screen.
  3. Tap Verify Code.
  4. No code yet? Wait for the timer, then tap Resend code.

Log in & reset your password

Getting back in — and what to do if you forget your password.

  1. On the Welcome back screen, enter your email and password, then tap Login.
  2. Or tap Continue with Google.
  3. Forgot it? Tap Forgot Password?, enter your email, and tap Send Verification Code.
  4. Use the emailed code to set a new password, then log in.

Meet your coach (onboarding)

A short chat where your AI coach gets to know you and builds your profile.

  1. On the welcome screen, tap Get Started.
  2. Answer the coach's questions in the chat — your style, interests, and what you're working on.
  3. When it's done, you'll see a “You're all set!” summary with your profile (personality, interests, and more).
  4. Tap Let's Go! to enter your coach. (A dark theme is available too.)

Set your first goal

A clear goal gives your coach something to steer toward.

  1. If you don't have an active goal yet, tap Create Your Goal.
  2. Define what you want to achieve and over what period.
  3. Your coach uses the goal to recommend the right sessions.
  4. You'll then track your Streak, Days Left, and Momentum against it.

Your home screen

Your daily command center — goal, today's session, commitments, and mood.

  1. Open the Home tab to see your current goal with its streak, days left, and momentum.
  2. Tap the Recommended Session for right now (e.g. Morning Session, 7–9 AM).
  3. Work through Today's Commitments and check each one off as you go.
  4. Log how you feel in Today's Pulse (Rough → Flow) — your coach references it next session.

Coaching sessions

Structured sessions for each part of your day, plus 24/7 quick help.

  1. Pick a session type: Morning (start focused), Evening (review & prep tomorrow), Weekly, or Monthly — each unlocks at its scheduled time.
  2. Tap a session to start a guided chat with your coach.
  3. Reply naturally; you can Copy or Regenerate any response, or Edit your own.
  4. Need help between sessions? Use Instant Queries — available 24/7.

Goals & progress

Track your active goal and look back on completed ones.

  1. Open the Coach tab and switch between Active Goal and Goals.
  2. Your active goal shows Streak, Days Left, and Momentum, plus its recommended sessions.
  3. The Goals list shows every goal with its duration and status.
  4. Tap any goal to open its details and sessions.

The Mentor

A second voice that adapts its style to how you need to be met today.

  1. Open the Mentor tab.
  2. Choose how you want to be met — Strategist (sharp, analytical), Guide (warm, reflective), or Challenger (direct, provoking).
  3. Read the AI's suggested style, based on your recent activity.
  4. Tap Continue to start your mentor chat. (Mentor chats use credits — see Account.)

Daily habits

Build the small routines that compound over time.

  1. Open the Habit tab and tap + to add a habit.
  2. Name it, and set how often and at what time it should happen.
  3. Mark each habit done on its days to grow your streak.
  4. Tap a habit to open Habit Details — a calendar where you mark each day Done, Missed, or Skipped, with your streak and overall rate.

Session history & commitments

Everything you've committed to, in one place.

  1. Open Session History to see your commitments grouped by date.
  2. Check off completed commitments.
  3. Use it to review what you've done across past sessions.

Account, credits & preferences

Manage your profile, balance, and settings.

  1. Open Account to see your available credits — tap Recharge Credits to top up.
  2. Manage your Subscription, set your Language, or toggle Dark Mode under Preferences.
  3. Find Privacy Policy and Export your data under Privacy.
  4. Tap Logout to sign out.

Plans & credits

Choose the plan that fits how much you'll use your coach and mentor.

  1. Open Choose a Plan and toggle Monthly or Yearly.
  2. Start with the Free Trial, or pick Starter / Growth / Power for more credits.
  3. Prefer no subscription? Scroll to Pay as you go for one-time credit packs.
  4. Tap Continue to confirm your choice.
