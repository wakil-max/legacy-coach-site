Getting started

From sign-up to your first shipped week.

Set up takes a few minutes. Here's exactly how to start using Legacy Foundry — step by step.

1

Apply for the beta

Drop your email to claim a founding-member seat. We onboard founders in small waves, so you'll get an invite to create your account.

10 seconds · free, no card
2

Install the app

Open your invite link, join the tester program, and install Legacy Foundry (Early Access) from Google Play.

~1 minute
3

Onboard with your coach

A short, friendly conversation where your AI coach learns about you and your business — so everything afterwards is tailored to you.

A few minutes
4

Set your first goal

Create one clear quarterly goal. This becomes the anchor your coach guides you toward every day.

A few minutes
5

Run your first morning session

Talk through your day with the coach. It helps you lock your top three moves — your most important actions, due by end of day.

Each morning
6

Close the loop & keep momentum

An evening session reviews the day, weekly and monthly sessions help you zoom out, and your habits and streaks keep you consistent.

Every day, week & month
