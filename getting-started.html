Home / Getting started

From sign-up to your first shipped week.

Legacy Foundry takes about five minutes to set up and a few minutes a day to use. Here's exactly what using it looks like, step by step — so you know what you're getting before you join.

Step 1 · Claim your spot

Apply for a founding-member seat.

Legacy Foundry is in private beta — we onboard founders in small waves so support stays personal. Drop your email to apply; approved founders get an invite to create their account. It's free during the beta, and founding members keep a permanent discount at launch.

  • ✓ Apply in 10 seconds with your email
  • ✓ Get your invite, then set a password
  • ✓ No credit card during beta

Step 2 · Onboard in 5 minutes

Tell Foundry about your business — once.

A short, guided onboarding maps your stage, your top goals, and how you work. This is what makes Foundry feel like a co-founder instead of a chatbot: it remembers your context, so you never re-explain your business again.

  • ✓ Pick your stage: idea, MVP, traction, scaling
  • ✓ Name your 1–3 real goals for the quarter
  • ✓ Set your working rhythm and capacity

Step 3 · Your morning forge

Wake up to three concrete moves.

Each morning, open Foundry to a short check-in. In about three minutes it reads your goals and yesterday's progress, then proposes the three moves that actually advance your business today. You adjust, commit, and go.

  • ✓ No blank page — the plan is already drafted
  • ✓ Tuned to your stage and your real goals
  • ✓ Commit in one tap and start your day

Step 4 · Evening close

Two minutes to close the loop.

At day's end, Foundry checks in: what shipped, what slipped, and why. It logs your blockers, protects tomorrow's focus, and keeps your streak alive — so the same thing doesn't stall you twice.

  • ✓ Mark what got done
  • ✓ Name the blocker, in one line
  • ✓ Tomorrow is pre-loaded for you

Step 5 · Weekly zoom-out

Watch your goals actually move.

Once a week, Foundry connects seven days of effort back to your goals, shows what's progressing, flags what's drifting, and recalibrates your priorities for the week ahead. Daily motion, in service of the bigger picture.

  • ✓ Recalibrate when priorities shift
