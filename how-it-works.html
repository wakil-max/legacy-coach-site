How it works

One goal. A daily rhythm. Real momentum.

Legacy Foundry turns a single quarterly goal into focused daily action — through short coaching sessions you simply talk your way through.

Try the beta — free →

It all starts with one quarterly goal.

You set a clear 13-week goal. Everything your coach does — every session, every daily move — is in service of moving that goal forward.

The rhythm

Your coach shows up on a cadence — you just talk to it.

No dashboards, no fixed clock. Each session is a short conversation — have it whenever your morning, evening, week or month calls for it.

☀️
Morning session

Set the day

Tell your coach how you're feeling and what's urgent. It helps you lock your top three moves for the day — your most important actions, tied to your goal.

🌙
Evening session

Review & reset

Review what got done, name what got in the way, and prepare tomorrow — so the same thing never stalls you twice.

🧭
Weekly session

Zoom out

A deeper review and planning session that connects your week of effort back to your goal and recalibrates priorities.

📅
Monthly session

Plan the month ahead

Step back, review real progress against your goal, and map out the month to come.

Session times are yours to set — morning, evening, weekly and monthly windows that fit your life. Times shown in the app are examples only.

Always on

And whenever you need more.

Beyond the daily rhythm, Legacy Foundry has your back between sessions.

🧠 On-demand Mentor

A strategic advisor you can summon any time. Choose how it meets you — Strategist (sharp), Guide (warm), or Challenger (direct).

🔁 Habit tracker

Build the daily habits behind your goal, with streaks and reminders that keep you consistent.

💬 24/7 Quick Chat

Instant support between sessions — ask a question or work through a blocker the moment it appears.
