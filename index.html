The founder's reality

You don't have a knowledge problem.
You have a follow-through problem.

Every founder knows what they "should" do. Doing it — alone, consistently, with no one watching — is the part that breaks you. That's the exact gap Legacy Foundry closes.

01

Wearing every hat

Product, sales, marketing, ops — all you. Context-switching grinds you down until burnout feels normal.

02

Decision fatigue

A hundred tiny choices drain you before you ever touch the work that moves the business.

03

No accountability

A human coach is $300/hour. Friends are too kind. Nobody checks whether you did what you said.

04

Goals quietly stall

The launch, the first ten customers, the first hire — they slip, because nothing connects today to the goal.

How you use it

Set up in 5 minutes. Use it in 5 a day.

Legacy Foundry is simple by design — apply, tell it about your business once, then let the daily rhythm do the work.

STEP 1

Apply & onboard

Claim a founding-member seat, then spend five minutes telling Foundry your stage, goals, and how you work.

STEP 2

Run the daily forge

Get three concrete moves each morning, work them, and close the loop at night — a few minutes, every day.

STEP 3

Watch goals move

Each week Foundry ties your effort back to your goals and recalibrates, so progress is visible — not vibes.
