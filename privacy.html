Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Legacy Foundry ("Legacy Foundry", "we", "us") is an AI coaching product for founders and entrepreneurs, operated by Legacy Ventures, part of the Onnorokom Group. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We believe in progress with conscience — and that includes how we treat your data.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us: your name, email, phone, region, role, company, startup stage, and what you're building — provided when you join the beta or create an account.
  • Coaching content: the goals, sessions, reflections, and messages you share with your AI coach in the app.
  • Usage information: basic, privacy-respecting analytics about how the website and app are used, to help us improve them.

How we use your information

  • To provide and personalise your coaching experience.
  • To send you your beta invite, account notifications, and important updates.
  • To improve our product, content, and support.
  • To keep our services secure and prevent abuse.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate (for example, hosting, email delivery, and authentication), under agreements that require them to protect it, or where required by law.

Data security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and access controls. No system is perfectly secure, but we work hard to safeguard your data and limit who can access it.

Your choices & rights

  • You can view and update your profile any time from your account.
  • You can request a copy of your data, or ask us to delete your account and associated data.
  • You can unsubscribe from non-essential emails using the link in any message.

Children's privacy

Legacy Foundry is intended for adults building businesses. It is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We'll revise the "last updated" date above and, for material changes, give you notice where appropriate.

Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email us at team@legacyfoundry.ai or visit our contact page.
